- Arrow Settings:
    + Arrow Gradient: Change the scrolling direction of the gradient on the frame, the body (middle part), the center,
    and/or the shine (only available in RGB, Vivid and CA Midi arrow variants) of the arrow.
    + Freeze Arrows: Switching this to On will make hold heads and roll heads freeze in one frame and become unanimated.
    
- Arrows: Change the arrow type/color scheme.
    + If you're looking for arrows that have the center animated in segments like original DDR, check out the CA (Center Animation) variants.
    + CA Alt is an alternate version of CA that slightly changes the way the gradient scrolls on the arrow body to be more accurate to DDR.
    + CA Midi is an alternate version of CA that makes the arrow look similar to the Midi noteskin (made by Midiman).
    + Modernized is the Shadow variant with tweaked texture sheets to imitate the ModernizedNote noteskin (made by FirstMaple8).
    
- Hold Explosions: Change the explosion type/color that shows up when a hold/roll is registered.

- Holds: Change the hold type/color.

- Mine Explosions: Change the explosion type/color that shows up when a mine is detonated.

- Mine Settings: (will not affect Shock Arrows)
    + Center Animation: Switching this to On and the center of mines will become animated.
    + Rotation: Switching this to Off will make the mine not rotate.
    
- Mines: Change the mine type/color.

- Receptor Pulse: Enable or disable the receptors flashing to the beat.

- Receptors: Change the receptor type/color.

- Rolls: Change the roll type/color.

- Tap Explosions: Change the explosion type/color scheme that shows up when a tap note is registered.
